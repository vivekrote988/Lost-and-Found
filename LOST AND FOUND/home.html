How it Works

When Found Any Lost Item

First click the picture of the lost item. Login into the Lost and Found web portal and go to the Lost and found listings and click on FOUND button then fill the form as given and Upload it.

When Lost Any Item

Login into the Lost and Found web portal and go to the Lost and found listings and click on LOST button then fill the form as given (if any image is available you can upload it ) and Upload the form.
